==== Use Cases ====
1. User Authentication and Profile Management:
-Focuses on user registration, login, and profile management.
-Essential for personalizing the user experience and securing user data.

2. Movie Browsing, Searching, and Actor/Cast Information:
-Combines movie and actor/cast searching, including viewing detailed information about movies and their associated cast and crew.
-Encompasses finding the highest rated and most recent movies for each actor/creator and displaying actor biographies.
-This unified use case offers a comprehensive view of the film database from both movie and actor perspectives.

3. User Interaction and Community Features:
-Includes recording and updating user preferences, watched movies, movie ratings (like/dislike), and comments.
-Covers the creation of user friends lists and facilitates community discussions in a forum-like setting for films and TV shows.
-Involves calculating links between movies and their cast or contributors, enhancing the social and interconnected aspect of the service.
